Tour Guides

In these tour guides, we will explore the hidden gems of Greece. This will include places like Milos, where we will visit the Plaka Castle, the Milos Mining Musuem, Tsigrado beach, Sarakinko beach, and the Papafragas Cave.

The next destination will be Symi, where we will look at Nanou beach, Marathounda beach, a boat ride to Agios Gerorgios Dysalona beach, Kali strata, and then the religious site - Panormitis Monastery.

The final destination wil be Athens, where we will see popular sites like the Monastiraki Flea Market, Areopagus, Bobo Winebar, the Benaki Museum, and Philopappos Hill.

Travel Tips

- Carry light luggage due to a lot of walking

- You will not need to book a rental car ahead of time

- Do not bring a lot of cash, use ATMS when you get there

- Give yourself a day in between your ferry and your flight home because they are not always on time and do not run all day

- Location matters! It is hard to find restaurants out of main cities and resorts/hotels
